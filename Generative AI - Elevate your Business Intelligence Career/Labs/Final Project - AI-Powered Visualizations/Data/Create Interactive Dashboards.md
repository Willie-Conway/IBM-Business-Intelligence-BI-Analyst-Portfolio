
# **Interactive Dashboard Design**

An interactive dashboard was designed to integrate all previously created visualizations into a single, cohesive view of business performance. The dashboard combines basic and advanced visualizations, including sales trends over time, category and regional performance, top-selling products, and geographic sales distribution.

### **Interactive Features Included**

* **Filters**

  * Region
  * Product Category
  * Order Year
    *Purpose:* Allows users to dynamically adjust the dashboard to focus on specific business segments or time periods.

* **Drill-Down Functionality**

  * Category → Sub-Category → Product
    *Purpose:* Enables users to move from high-level summaries to detailed product-level insights without leaving the dashboard.

* **Tooltips**

  * Display detailed metrics such as total sales, profit, quantity sold, and order count on hover
    *Purpose:* Provides additional context without cluttering the visual layout.

---

## **User Experience and Dashboard Flow**

The dashboard was designed with a logical, top-down flow to enhance usability:

1. **High-Level KPIs**

   * Total Sales
   * Total Profit
   * Number of Orders

2. **Performance Overview**

   * Sales over time (trend analysis)
   * Sales by category and region

3. **Detailed Insights**

   * Top-selling products
   * Geographic sales distribution
   * Advanced visualizations (heatmaps and bubble charts)

### **Steps Taken to Improve Usability**

* Consistent color themes across all visualizations
* Clear titles, labels, and legends
* Grouped visuals by analytical purpose
* Minimal clutter with whitespace for readability
* Intuitive filter placement at the top of the dashboard

These design choices ensure the dashboard is easy to navigate and understandable for both technical and non-technical users.
