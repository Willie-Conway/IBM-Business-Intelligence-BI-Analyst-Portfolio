### Business Insights

* **Copiers and printers dominate sales** within the Technology category, indicating strong demand for office hardware.
* The **Canon imageCLASS 2200** significantly outperforms all other products, suggesting it is a flagship product that should be prioritized for inventory availability.
* High-ticket technology items contribute disproportionately to revenue, highlighting opportunities for:

  * Focused marketing campaigns
  * Bundled service or warranty offerings
  * Strategic supplier partnerships

### Strategic Implications

* **Inventory planning:** Maintain higher stock levels for top-performing products to avoid lost sales.
* **Marketing strategy:** Promote best-sellers in targeted campaigns and upsell complementary accessories.
* **Product focus:** Use these insights to guide future product assortment and investment decisions.
