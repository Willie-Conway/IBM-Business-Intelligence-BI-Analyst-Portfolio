# Competitor Performance Analysis – Past Year

This analysis compares our company’s key performance metrics with those of a primary competitor over the past year, focusing on market share, revenue growth, and customer satisfaction.

## Market Share

Our company maintained a stable market share of **25%**, slightly up from 24% last year, driven by successful marketing campaigns and expansion into new regions. The key competitor holds a **30% market share**, increasing from 28%, benefiting from aggressive pricing and strong brand recognition.

## Revenue Growth

Our revenue grew by **10%**, rising from $500 million to $550 million, fueled by new product launches, strategic partnerships, and operational efficiencies. The competitor experienced a slightly higher growth of **12%**, increasing revenue from $600 million to $672 million, due to innovation and expansion into emerging markets.

## Customer Satisfaction

Customer satisfaction remains a priority. Our company achieved an **85% satisfaction score**, up 3% from last year, reflecting improved customer service and faster response times. The competitor’s satisfaction score is **88%**, up from 86%, supported by investments in customer experience technologies.

## Summary

Both companies demonstrated positive trends over the past year. While the competitor slightly outperformed in revenue growth and market share, our company shows strong improvements in customer satisfaction and operational efficiency. Future focus will be on leveraging these strengths to drive further growth and market share gains.
