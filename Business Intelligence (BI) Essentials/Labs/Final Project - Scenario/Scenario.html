💡 Key Insights & Recommendations

✅ Top Performing Segment

Technology products generate 38% of total revenue with copiers and phones having the highest profit margins (35%+). Recommend increasing inventory and marketing focus on these categories.

⚠️ Underperforming Category

Furniture sales show only 18% profit margin, significantly below company average. Tables and bookcases are particularly problematic with negative margins in some regions.

📈 Growth Opportunity

Office supplies show consistent growth (15% YoY) with stable margins. Recommend expanding the premium office supplies line in Western states where demand is highest.

🚨 Regional Concern

Midwestern states underperform by 22% compared to coastal regions. Michigan and Ohio show declining sales in furniture and electronics. Recommend regional promotional campaigns.

🎯 Strategic Recommendations

1. Product Strategy

• Increase inventory allocation for Technology products by 25%
• Review and renegotiate Furniture supplier contracts
• Introduce premium office supplies line in high-performing regions

2. Regional Focus

• Launch targeted marketing campaigns in Midwestern states
• Expand distribution centers in California and Texas
• Analyze competitor pricing in underperforming regions

3. Operational Improvements

• Implement dynamic pricing for furniture products
• Enhance online shopping experience for office supplies
• Train sales team on high-margin product features
